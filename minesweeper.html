Welcome to Minesweeper Brought to you by: Brayden Cleary

There are 10 mines and 64 total tiles.
  • Click to uncover tile
  • Click + alt-key to 'flag' a tile (blue background)
  • Click + alt-key a flagged tile to 'question mark' a tile (pink background)
  • Reveal mines by clicking 'toggle cheat' (red background)

Ways to win:
  1. Clicking on all non-mine (54) tiles.
  2. Flagging all the mines and clicking the 'check' button.
